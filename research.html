Research

VR Rehabilitation Coach

Unity and Meta Quest research project for upper-limb bicep curl rehabilitation, real-time feedback, and CSV movement logging.

Project Objective

Develop a Unity-based VR rehab app where a user grabs a virtual dumbbell, performs bicep curls, and receives live feedback on movement quality.

Unity Meta Quest Bicep Curl CSV Logging

Core Features

  • Clean VR rehab room scene.
  • Grabbable virtual dumbbell.
  • Quest headset/controller tracking.
  • Rep counter and movement-quality score.
  • Green/yellow/red feedback cues.
  • CSV export for research data.
